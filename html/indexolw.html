All Levels at Once - Our Little World Lyrics

Hello, my name is Riley Trautman and I decided that I wanted to make a simple website to try to teach myself htlm and css. This website is a result of that.

Beyond this goal of making a website, I also wanted it to serve some sort of purpose. I decided to dedicate this website to hosting the lyrics of the songs of Our Little World by All Levels at Once. Our Little World is a wonderful EP and I would definitely recommend purchasing/downloading it. I have included many links to it, on the page for each song, there is a link to the individual song on Bandcamp, and there is a link on the top of this page to the album, as well as a link to the album on every page disguised as an album cover.
