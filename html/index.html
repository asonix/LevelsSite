All Levels at Once - Tesselate Lyrics

Hello, my name is Riley Trautman and I decided that I wanted to make a simple website to try to teach myself htlm and css. This website is a result of that.

Beyond this goal of making a website, I also wanted it to serve some sort of purpose. I decided to dedicate this website to hosting the lyrics of the songs of Tesselate by All Levels at Once. Tesselate is a wonderful album and I would definitely recommend purchasing it. I have included many links to it, on the page for each song, there is a link to the individual song on Bandcamp, and there is a link on the top of this page to the album, as well as a link to the album on every page disguised as an album cover.
